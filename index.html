About Us

A Stage for Everyone

The Society of Performing Arts at Taylor's University (SPART) is a student-led organization dedicated to fostering creativity in the performing arts. We provide a platform for students to explore theatre in all its facets.

Workshops & Training

We cultivate a dynamic community through workshops, performances, and collaborative projects.

Original Productions

We produce original plays and adaptations across all genres, giving students opportunities in acting, directing, writing, and design.

A Role for Everyone

We are a community of creatives where everyone has a role to play in bringing powerful stories to life.
